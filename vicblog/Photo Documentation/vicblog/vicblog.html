Final Reflections

Key Learnings at PNP Sorsogon

My 12-week OJT experience at the PNP Sorsogon CPS has been incredibly transformative. Working in a law enforcement IT environment taught me not only technical skills but also the importance of security, confidentiality, and serving the public interest.

Technical Skills Developed

Network Troubleshooting

Diagnosing and resolving connectivity issues, switch configuration,

Hardware Support

Computer repairs, component replacement, peripheral setup,Printer system configuration

Graphic Design

Social media cards creation using Canva and Photoshop, public awareness materials, infographics

Event Management

Seminar planning, A/V setup, logistics coordination, technical support during events

Social Media Management

Content creation, campaign planning, community engagement, digital communications

Documentation

Technical writing, procedure guides, knowledge base creation, inventory management

Most Memorable Experiences

Seminar Success: Coordinating and supporting a seminar for police officers was my proudest achievement. From planning to execution, seeing everything come together and receiving positive feedback was incredibly rewarding.
Critical Network Issue Resolution: When the entire office lost network connectivity due to a switch failure, I helped diagnose and resolve the issue. The experience taught me to stay calm under pressure and think systematically.
Social Media Impact: Creating awareness campaigns that reached thousands of people in Sorsogon showed me how IT skills can directly contribute to public safety and community welfare.

Personal Growth

Working at PNP Sorsogon CPS taught me professionalism, discipline, and the value of public service. The structured environment and high standards of the police organization pushed me to always deliver quality work. I learned that technical skills are important, but soft skills like communication, teamwork, and adaptability are equally crucial.

Gratitude

I'm deeply grateful to the PNP Sorsogon CPS especially the IT Department, for giving me this opportunity. My supervisors were patient mentors who shared their expertise generously. This experience has prepared me not just for a career in IT, but for contributing meaningfully to society.

Moving Forward

The skills and values I've gained at PNP Sorsogon CPS will guide my future career. I'm now confident in handling technical challenges, managing projects, and working in high-stakes environments. I'm excited to continue learning and growing as an IT professional who can make a positive difference.

Thank You
